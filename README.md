# ZOOMIES

## Summary

ZOOMIES is a colorful endless runner built for Apple Watch. Choose a character and map, jump over obstacles, collect coins, and chase your best distance.

## Features

- Multiple playable characters and themed maps
- Standard and one-life YOLO modes
- Coin collection, persistent totals, and best-distance tracking
- Sound, haptic, control, and distance-unit settings
- Animated watchOS interface

## Current Implementations

- Five playable characters: Cappi, Pebble, Biscuit, Boba, and Momo
- Five maps: Meadow Valley, Frost Peak, Cozy Town, Sunny Beach, and Jungle Canopy
- Endless-runner gameplay with jumping, obstacles, coins, collisions, lives, and increasing difficulty
- Character abilities including double jump, ice slide, coin-streak boost, and banana magnet
- Standard mode and one-hit YOLO mode with a score multiplier
- Screen-button and Digital Crown control options
- Animated menus, gameplay backgrounds, character badges, audio, and haptic feedback
- Persistent best distances, YOLO best distance, total coins, character stats, selections, and settings
